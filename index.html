Our Story

Cozy, traditional, and made the home-style way.

Panaderia La Buena is a family-owned neighborhood Mexican panaderia bringing traditional Mexican bread and pastries to San Diego. Our recipes follow home-style Villanueva, Zacatecas-style traditions, favoring authenticity over trends, and warmth over flash.

From fresh conchas and bolillos to pan dulce trays and custom cakes, everything is made the way panaderias have always made it: with care, and for the neighborhood.

Good To Know

Frequently Asked Questions

A few honest answers before your first visit.

Do you take custom cake orders?

Yes. Panaderia La Buena makes custom cakes (pasteles) for birthdays, quinceañeras, weddings, baptisms, anniversaries, and other special occasions. Call (619) 696-0611 to discuss your order, the more lead time you can give, the better.

What are your hours?

The bakery is open 6:00 AM to 9:00 PM Monday through Saturday, and 6:00 AM to 3:30 PM on Sunday. Since small bakeries can occasionally adjust hours around holidays, it's always smart to call ahead if you're making a special trip.

Do you sell pan dulce by the piece or only in trays?

Traditional panaderias like ours typically sell pan dulce both individually and in larger trays for parties or families, alongside daily favorites like conchas and bolillos. Ask at the counter for what's fresh and available that day.

Is parking available?

The bakery is a small neighborhood storefront on Imperial Avenue near the Market Street and 26th Street area. Street parking is typically available nearby; as with any small shop on a busy avenue, spots can be tighter during peak hours.

Do you take special dietary requests?

For any allergy, dietary restriction, or special recipe request, the best step is to call (619) 696-0611 and ask directly, the team can tell you what's possible for a given order.
